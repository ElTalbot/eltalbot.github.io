About Me

Why an interest in technology?

I was looking for a way to channel my passion for building, creativity, helping people and fell into the world of tech.

The founding of my business along with my experience in the dynamic world of a software company startup highlighted the power of tech and its potential to drive my business further.

Working in the world of technology was something that never seemed possible for me. Being given the opportunity to foster this newfound interest and be able to use it for good is something I want to nurture.

What excites me the most about working in the tech industry?

Having the opportunity to collaborate with other like-minded people to build, create and develop in a way that has a positive impact in society.

The ongoing changes and advancements in the world of software brings with it new possibilities, keeping everyday exciting and different.

The brevity of scope the tech industry has, the collaboration and possibilities right round the corner that offer the potential to influence the future of technology are things that really excite me.

The reach of tech - being apart of an industry that has a positive impact in a quiet low key way, be it for your local neighbour or for a community on the other side of the world is something I strive to be apart of.

What do you hope to achieve or contribute to in your future endeavors?

Two things.

1. To bring what I have learnt and make a difference professionally - to work alongside others to build and code with passion, integrity and respect whilst not forgetting to have fun

2. The opportunity to collaborate with other passionate people on a more personal level and see the evolution of an idea right from conception all the way to deployment.
